My Favorite City: Rosario, Santa Fe

My favorite city is Rosario, in Santa Fe, Argentina. I think it is a beautiful city because it has many parks, interesting buildings, and a great view of the Parana River. I also like its public spaces and the old part of downtown, where you can see different architectural styles.


One interesting fact about Rosario is that it is closely related to the Argentine flag. The flag was raised there for the first time, and the city has the National Flag Memorial. It is a very beautiful monument with sculptures and important historical meaning. It also represents the importance of the Parana River for the region and its port activity.
